Welcome to Don Bosco Technical High School

Don Bosco Technical High School is located on 8th Street, Sinkor, Monrovia, Liberia. The school helps students build strong academic knowledge and useful technical skills for the future.

Learn More

Our School

Don Bosco Technical High School gives young people the opportunity to learn, grow, and become responsible citizens. The school focuses on discipline, hard work, creativity, and practical learning.

Quality Education

Students are taught important subjects that prepare them for higher education and real life.

Technical Training

The school offers practical skills such as electricity, solar, sewing, and other technical courses.

Student Activities

Students take part in sports, Mass, workshops, quizzing, debate, and school programs.
