Who We Are

Don Bosco Technical High School is a learning institution in Sinkor, Monrovia. The school is known for helping students develop good character, discipline, and technical skills.

Our school believes that education should prepare students for both the classroom and the real world. Students are encouraged to work hard, respect others, and use their talents positively.

Mission

To provide quality education and technical training that help students become responsible, skilled, and productive citizens.

Vision

To become one of the best technical high schools in Liberia by preparing students for success in school, work, and society.

Motto

Laborare ET Liberare, which reminds students about the value of work, discipline, and service.
